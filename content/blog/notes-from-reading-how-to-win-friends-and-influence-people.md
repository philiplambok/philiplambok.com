I finished reading How to Win Friends & Influence People in January 2026. I want to share the notes I wrote while reading it.

As the title says, this book is about social skills. It talks about how to deal with people, make people like you, make people agree with you, and be a good leader.

## Tips for dealing with people

There are two main tips:

- Avoid blame and criticism. Anyone can criticize or complain, but it takes character to understand and forgive.
- Give honest appreciation. People want to feel important and needed.

## Tips for making people like you

There are four tips:

- Smile.
- Remember their name.
- Be a good listener.
  Encourage them to talk about themselves.
- Talk about what interests them.

## Tips for making people agree with you

There are six tips:

- Avoid pointless arguments.
- Respect other people's opinions.
  Do not say, "You're wrong!"
- If you are wrong, admit it.
- Start the conversation in a kind and friendly way.
- Let ideas feel like they come from them.
- Try to see things from the other person's point of view.

## Tips for being a good leader

There are seven tips:

- Start by giving honest praise so the other person feels appreciated.
- Point out mistakes in a gentle way.
- Admit your own mistakes before correcting others.
- Ask questions instead of giving orders. For example, instead of saying, “Do it like this,” you can say, “How about we do it like this?”
- Treat others in a way that does not embarrass them.
- Praise every improvement, even the small ones.
- Support them and show that mistakes can be fixed.

If you want to go deeper, I recommend reading the book.
