---
title: "Why I Write"
date: "2026-03-13"
description: "On the value of writing as a software engineer."
---

Writing helps me think. When I try to explain something in writing, I discover the gaps in my understanding. It forces clarity.

As a software engineer, most of my work is about communicating ideas — to other developers, to future me, to the computer. Writing is just another form of that practice.

This blog is my space to work through ideas in public. Some posts will be polished, others will be rough. That's fine. The point is to keep writing.
